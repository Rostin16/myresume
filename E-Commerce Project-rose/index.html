Our Story

RosTin gems is a British family-run jewellery brand creating unique sterling silver jewellery inspired by art, architecture, and nature.
Each piece is individually designed by Paula, who likes to think of her jewellery as wearable works of art. Paula’s jewellery collections for prestigious museums, galleries and royal houses have become collectable by discerning customers worldwide.
Since we began in 1994, our jewellery has been crafted ethically by the same Balinese family of Silversmiths.
